Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026 · Keyweaver Ltd · ICO registration: ZC174255 · Questions: privacy@keyweaver.io

Plain English summary

  • We process your audio to generate captions using OpenAI Whisper. Audio is not stored after transcription.
  • We store the transcript text and timestamps so you can re-generate styles without re-uploading.
  • We do not sell your data. We do not use your content to train AI models.
  • We use Supabase (database), Stripe (payments), and Vercel (hosting). All are GDPR-compliant.
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your account page.
  • Marketing emails are optional at signup — we only email you about product news if you opt in.

2. What data we collect

Account data

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (stored as a bcrypt hash — we never see your plaintext password)
  • Account creation date
  • Subscription tier and credit balance
  • Marketing preferences — only if you tick the optional checkbox at signup (first name, email address, consent date, and consent wording version)

Audio and transcript data

When you generate captions, the Cuemark plugin sends your audio file to our backend, which forwards it to the OpenAI Whisper API for transcription. Here is exactly what happens:

Data Stored? How long?
Audio file No — passed directly to Whisper, never written to disk on our servers Not stored
Transcript text Yes — stored in your account so you can re-apply styles without re-uploading Until you delete the job or your account
Word timestamps Yes — stored alongside transcript text Until you delete the job or your account
File name and duration Yes — stored for your generate history Until you delete the job or your account
Language detected Yes — stored for reference Until you delete the job or your account

Important: OpenAI's API terms state that audio submitted via the API is not used to train their models. We recommend reviewing OpenAI's API data usage policy for the latest information.

Usage data

We collect basic usage information including:

  • Number of generates, credits consumed, and job status (complete / failed)
  • Errors and crash reports to help us fix bugs
  • Plugin version in use

We use the Meta Pixel on keyweaver.io to measure visits and ad performance from Meta (Facebook/Instagram) campaigns. Meta may set cookies or similar identifiers in your browser. See Meta’s privacy policy at facebook.com/privacy/policy. We use Google Ads conversion tracking (gtag.js) to measure visits and conversions from Google Ads campaigns. Google may set cookies such as _gcl_au. See Google’s privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Payment data

Payments are handled by Stripe. We never see or store your card number, CVC, or full payment details. Stripe provides us with a customer reference, subscription status, and invoice history. Stripe's privacy policy is at stripe.com/gb/privacy.

5. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction — ask us to limit processing while a dispute is resolved
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — opt out of marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@keyweaver.io. We will respond within 30 days. You can also delete your account directly from your account page, which removes all personal data immediately.

You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

6. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your profile, transcripts, and job history are deleted immediately
  • Billing records are retained for 7 years as required by UK law
  • Stripe retains payment data under their own retention policy

Inactive accounts (no login for 24 months) will receive an email notice and be deleted 30 days later unless you sign in.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a minimum cost factor of 12
  • Database access is restricted by row-level security policies
  • Session tokens are 128-character cryptographically random hex strings
  • API keys and secrets are stored in environment variables, never in code

If you discover a security vulnerability, please contact us at security@keyweaver.io before disclosing it publicly.

8. Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies:

  • Authentication — a session token stored in localStorage (not a cookie) to keep you signed in. This is strictly necessary.
  • Meta Pixel — we use Meta’s pixel on the website for ad measurement; see our Cookie Policy.

Because we use no non-essential cookies, no cookie consent banner is required. See our Cookie Policy for more detail.

9. Children

Cuemark is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us at privacy@keyweaver.io and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified by email at least 14 days before they take effect. The "last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the current version.
