Cookie Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026 · Privacy Policy

This policy explains how keyweaver.io and Keyweaver After Effects plugins (Cuemark, Superconductor, Trillian, Tamborine, Ludo, and Keyweaver Manager where applicable) use cookies and similar browser storage.

What we use

Name / keyWherePurposeDuration
cc_tokenWebsite & panelsKeeps you signed in to your Keyweaver accountUntil logout or expiry
cc_web_device_idWebsiteDevice fingerprint for session security on web loginPersistent
captio_device_fp (and similar panel device keys)Keyweaver panels in After EffectsDevice fingerprint for panel login / session securityPersistent
cuemark_cookie_consentWebsiteRemembers your cookie choice (accept or reject marketing)Until cleared
Panel UI preferences (localStorage)Individual panelsRemembers layout and tool settings on your machineUntil cleared
Meta Pixel (_fbp, _fbc, etc.)WebsiteMarketing measurement for Meta campaigns - only if you accept optional cookiesVaries (set by Meta)
Google Ads (_gcl_au, etc.)WebsiteMeasures ad performance and conversions from Google Ads - only if you acceptVaries (set by Google)

What we do not use

  • General analytics suites beyond the ad measurement tags listed above
  • Social media embed trackers

Consent

Strictly necessary storage (sign-in and security, plus your saved cookie preference) does not require consent under UK/EU rules.

Optional marketing tags (Meta Pixel and Google Ads) are non-essential and load only after you click Accept all on the cookie banner. If you click Reject non-essential, those tags are not loaded. You can change your choice later via Cookie settings in the footer.

You can change your choice at any time using Cookie settings in the site footer.

Managing storage

Sign out from the website or any Keyweaver panel to clear your session token. You can also clear site data in your browser settings, or clear CEP/extension storage for After Effects panels. Clearing storage will sign you out for that browser or panel instance.
