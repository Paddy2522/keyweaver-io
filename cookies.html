Cookie Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026 · Privacy Policy

This policy explains how keyweaver.io and the Cuemark CEP panel use cookies and similar browser storage.

What we use

Name / keyWherePurposeDuration
cc_tokenWebsite & panelKeeps you signed in to your Keyweaver accountUntil logout or expiry
cc_web_device_idWebsiteDevice fingerprint for session security on web loginPersistent
captio_device_fpCuemark panel (After Effects)Device fingerprint for panel loginPersistent

What we do not use

  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Third-party analytics trackers (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, etc.)
  • Social media embed trackers

Consent

We only use strictly necessary storage for authentication and security. Under UK/EU rules, this does not require a consent banner. If we add optional analytics in future, we will update this policy and ask for consent first.

Managing storage

Sign out from the website or panel to clear your session token. You can also clear site data in your browser settings. Clearing storage will sign you out everywhere for that browser or panel instance.
