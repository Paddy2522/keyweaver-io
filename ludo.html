Features

Built for real edit sessions

Animation & keys

Easing, strength, stagger, align, reverse, distribute, and mirror across selected keys.

Text & layout

Split characters, typewriter-style tools, stroke boxes, cloner, and effectors.

Colour palette

Save swatches, sample from the comp, and apply to text, solids, shapes, and lights.

Export helpers

Export frames or a work-area GIF without digging through the Render Queue.
