Can You Add Automatic Subtitles in After Effects?

Yes — but not with a built-in Adobe feature. After Effects does not include native speech-to-text. Automatic subtitles in AE come from third-party plugins that transcribe your comp audio and build timed caption layers on your timeline.

Short answer: yes, with a plugin

If you searched "can I add automatic subtitles in After Effects", the answer is yes — automatic subtitles are possible when you use an AI caption plugin installed as a CEP panel in After Effects.

The workflow looks like this:

  1. Open your comp with dialogue or voiceover.
  2. Run transcription from the plugin panel (cloud AI).
  3. Review and edit the transcript.
  4. Apply styled text layers to your timeline automatically.

No manual typing. No frame-by-frame trimming for every line — the AI returns word-level timestamps.

Why doesn't After Effects have native auto-captions?

Adobe added speech-to-text captions in Premiere Pro, aimed at editorial timelines and broadcast delivery. After Effects is a compositing and motion graphics tool — Adobe has not shipped an equivalent transcription engine inside AE.

That gap is filled by the plugin ecosystem. Motion designers who finish in AE need transcription inside the comp, not in a separate NLE.

The Premiere Pro speech-to-text alternative

Some editors transcribe in Premiere, export SRT, and import into After Effects. This works but adds steps:

  • Round-tripping between apps breaks a single-timeline workflow
  • SRT import rarely preserves styled backgrounds or word animations
  • Re-timing after picture changes means re-exporting subtitles

If your entire edit lives in After Effects, in-app transcription saves time and keeps captions editable on the same timeline.

Frequently asked questions

Can After Effects automatically generate subtitles?

Not natively. After Effects has no built-in speech-to-text for subtitles. You can add automatic subtitles using third-party plugins such as Cuemark that transcribe comp audio and create timed text layers inside AE.

Is there speech to text in After Effects?

Adobe has added speech-to-text in Premiere Pro, not in After Effects. AE users typically use caption plugins, import SRT from Premiere, or transcribe with dedicated AI tools built for After Effects.

How accurate are automatic subtitles in After Effects?

Accuracy depends on audio quality and the transcription engine. Clean dialogue often reaches 90–95% accuracy. Always review and fix names, jargon, and punctuation before final delivery.

Do automatic subtitles cost money in After Effects?

Cloud transcription uses credits in tools like Cuemark — typically one credit per minute of audio. Re-styling and re-applying cached transcripts does not use additional credits.
