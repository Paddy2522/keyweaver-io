How it works

Generate in three steps

Everything happens inside After Effects. No browser tabs, no exports, no re-linking.

01

Open your edit comp

Open the composition with your video or audio in After Effects. Cuemark reads layers in the active comp and shows credit cost before you transcribe.

02

Generate Subtitles

One click transcribes your audio in the cloud. Word-level timestamps come back and are cached for free re-wraps and style changes.

03

Style in the panel

Native AE text layers land in your timeline - timed, editable, with presets and animations applied from the Cuemark panel.

Style library

Caption presets, built in After Effects

Every preset is a real AE style - font, colour, animation, and background. Change anything in the Style tab after applying.

Make it yours

Total control over every caption

Start from a preset, then change anything. Your fonts, your colours, your look - applied as real After Effects layers you can keep editing.

Use your own fonts

Apply any font installed on your system - brand typefaces, custom display fonts, anything After Effects can render. Set weight and size to match your project.

Custom colours

Pick exact text, background, and highlight colours. Match a brand palette or a video's grade - nothing is locked to the preset you started from.

Backgrounds & shapes

Add a pill or box background behind captions, or turn it off entirely. Control padding and opacity so text stays readable over any footage.

Position & alignment

Place captions where you want them - top, centre, or lower third - with alignment and size controls that update live on the timeline.

Edit text & punctuation

Fix transcripts line by line in the Text tab - wording, casing, and punctuation - before applying. The AI gets you 95% there; you polish the rest.

Export anywhere

Need captions outside After Effects? Export to SRT, VTT, or CSV for YouTube, social platforms, or your review workflow.

Re-style any time, free

Your transcript is cached, so changing fonts, colours, presets, or re-wrapping lines costs no extra credits. Experiment until it's right.

Word-level highlights

Highlight the active word as it's spoken with spotlight, karaoke, and impact styles - the punchy look short-form viewers expect.

Motion library

Caption animations, word by word

Character and word-level motion - spotlight, karaoke, typewriter, and more. Apply from the Style tab after generating subtitles.
